About

Voting is a method for a group, such as a meeting or an electorate, in order to make a collective decision or express an opinion usually following discussions, debates or election campaigns, Democracies elect holders of high office by voting. Residents of a place represented by elected official are called constituents, and those constituents who cast a ballot for their chosen candidate are called voters. There are different system for collecting votes, but while many of the systems used in the decision-making can also be used as electoral system, any which cater for proportional representatives can only be used in elections.
